Gin Fizz

Description

This recipe is adapted from this recipe from acouplecooks.com

It's gin fizz o'clock somewhere! Enjoy this classic cocktail, a fizzy, foamy, sweet gin drink. Pair it with a good soul tune and you're set!

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz gin
  • 3/4 oz lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz maple syrup
  • 1 egg white
  • Soda water

Steps:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, shake gin, lemon juice, syrup, and egg white vigorously for 15 seconds.
  2. Fill the shaker with ice and shake for 30 seconds until chilled.
  3. Strain the drink into a glass and top with soda water. The foam will form on top.
